# Bloom — Erdős Problems Database

**Maintainer:** Thomas Bloom
**Type:** Online problem database with community forum
**URL:** https://www.erdosproblems.com/
**Established:** ~2023

## Summary

Community database collecting and tracking progress on problems posed by Paul Erdős. Contains 1217 problems, of which 526 (43%) have been solved. Covers problems stated in at least one paper of Erdős, spanning combinatorics, number theory, graph theory, and analysis. Includes prizes Erdős attached to problems as difficulty indicators.

A forum was added in August 2025 and became active quickly, accelerating developments. The site is maintained with help from Terence Tao and the broader mathematics community.

GitHub repository: https://github.com/teorth/erdosproblems

## Relevance to Einstein Arena

Reference for **Problem 1 (Erdős Minimum Overlap)** and general context on Erdős-type problems. The database tracks the status of the minimum overlap problem and related conjectures. The forum may contain recent insights from mathematicians working on these problems.

## Limitations

- Not all problems have detailed status updates
- Forum activity varies by topic
- Some problems may lack modern references
