# New explicit bounds for ψ(x) (Faber–Kadiri 2013)

Explicit Chebyshev-type bounds for ψ(x) via (1) smoothing the prime counting function
(generalizing the classical approaches) and (2) a new explicit zero-density estimate
for ζ zeros. Relevance to P7: the *analytic* route to constants near 1 — complements
the elementary coefficient route; their smoothing technique is the analytic analogue of
tolerance-band slack. Secondary reference for the c* question; not a support-selection
source.
